Here is a list of major Red Boost Vitamins & Ingredients along with their benefits: 

1

Beetroot

Beetroot is the primary ingredient in Red Boost. It is rich in nitrates, which are converted into nitric oxide in the body. This process improves blood flow and oxygen delivery, leading to increased energy levels and improved physical performance.
2

Green Tea Extract

Green Tea Extract is known for its antioxidant properties, which can help to protect the body from damage by free radicals. It also has the potential to boost metabolism, supporting weight loss and overall health.
3

Rhodiola Rosea

Rhodiola Rosea is a plant that has been used in traditional medicine for its potential to reduce fatigue and enhance mental performance. It is believed to help the body adapt to physical and mental stress.
4

Vitamin B12

Vitamin B12 is essential for the proper function and development of the brain, nerves, and blood cells. It can help to prevent anemia, enhance mood, and boost energy levels.
5

Vitamin B6

Vitamin B6 plays a crucial role in brain development and function. It is involved in the production of the neurotransmitters serotonin and norepinephrine, which regulate mood and stress responses.
6

Folic Acid

Folic Acid, also known as Vitamin B9, is vital for the production of DNA and RNA, the body's genetic material. It is especially important during periods of rapid cell division and growth, such as during pregnancy and infancy.
7

Magnesium

Magnesium is a mineral that is important for many processes in the body, including regulating muscle and nerve function, blood sugar levels, and blood pressure. It is also needed for making protein, bone, and DNA.
8

Zinc

Zinc is an essential mineral that is involved in numerous aspects of cellular metabolism. It is necessary for the function of over 300 enzymes and is involved in many important processes in the body, including immune function, protein synthesis, wound healing, DNA synthesis, and cell division.
9

Selenium

Selenium is a trace mineral that is essential for good health but required only in small amounts. Selenium is incorporated into proteins to make selenoproteins, which are important antioxidant enzymes.
10

Chromium

Chromium is a mineral that enhances the function of insulin, a hormone critical to the metabolism and storage of carbohydrate, fat, and protein in the body. It may also be beneficial for weight management and mood regulation.
